Bitcoin Market Analysis & Price Movement Prediction

Time-series analysis and machine learning applied to cryptocurrency markets

Project Overview

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and influenced by numerous economic, technical, and behavioral factors. This project focuses on analyzing historical Bitcoin price data to understand market behavior and predict future price movements using data science and machine learning techniques.

Problem Statement

The key challenge is determining whether historical Bitcoin price data can be used to identify meaningful patterns that help predict future price movements. Due to high volatility and correlated variables, feature selection and model evaluation play a critical role in building reliable predictions.

Data Understanding & Exploratory Analysis

The dataset consists of historical open, high, low, and close (OHLC) price values. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was performed to examine trends over time, analyze distributions, and study correlations between variables.

Key Visual Analysis

Visual analysis was performed to understand relationships between variables and evaluate model performance. The correlation heatmap highlights dependencies among price features, while the confusion matrix evaluates the predictive accuracy of the predictive model.
