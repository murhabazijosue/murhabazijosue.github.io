Bike Ride Demand Forecasting & Scenario-Based Prediction

Machine learning–based demand prediction using historical and contextual data

Project Overview

Ride-hailing and bike-sharing platforms operate in dynamic environments where demand fluctuates based on time, weather, seasonality, and human behavior. Accurate demand forecasting is essential for efficient resource allocation.

Problem Statement

The challenge is predicting bike ride demand under varying conditions. Poor demand estimation can result in idle vehicles, longer wait times, and inefficient operations.

Data Understanding & Feature Engineering

Historical ride data was enriched with weather and time-based features. Feature engineering captured demand cycles and environmental influence.

Prediction Interface & Scenario Analysis

Challenges & Key Learnings

  • Capturing non-linear demand patterns
  • Handling seasonality and peak periods
  • Balancing accuracy and interpretability
  • Translating predictions into business insight

Tools & Technologies

Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib
